Sand can be harvest with out any tool and its effected by gravity.

If there is non solid Block (Torch, Rail etc.) under it will drop.

This can  be useful if you want to farm Sand, Gravel or Red Sand.

Sometime its happened that Sand is Generated without an "Normal"

Block under it. 


Sand can be smelted into glass.
[furnace{200,50,block/sand.red}block/glass]
[furnace{200,200,key=block/glass}]
